Big Data in Agriculture

Big data plays an influential role in agriculture to improve firms efficiency. The goal is to minimize the loss of the firm and increase the generation of needed food grains for the nation's citizens. Data science has contributed a great deal to integrating new and modern approaches into current agricultural practices. Big data uses allow us to meet the required quantity of annual production and also eliminate the need to import goods.
